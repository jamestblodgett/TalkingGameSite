The Characters

My game has dozens of unique characters, each with their own personalities, appearances, backstories, and dozens of other traits. It is simply a lot to unpack, so I will need to organize this orderly and well.

I will organize the characters based on the groups they are associated with. For example, allies of one player would be in one group, while people different groups would have different people listed with them. It's important to be patient as I add the rest of these characters.

Gods, Judges, "Cartoon Characters", and bonded beings, such as spren and symblings, will not be listed on this page.
