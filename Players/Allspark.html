Allspark

Names

  • Allspark

    chosen as a name meant to mean “All powers” or similar, was originally associated with the ability to gain more powers when the current one is mastered.

  • The Nameless King

    a king - due to his repeated ruling of the old world, level 1, and being a king, but also related to his void-related powers that let him hide his identity. The name was originally chosen as a pseudonym for him to fake his death and manipulate the other players, and the meanings related to it are almost perfectly coincidental.

  • The Eye

    A name used by others, due to his magical eye.

Personal-made Lore

(The following was written by the person who plays Allspark)

Allspark was once named Clarence the last blade. He is unaware of this. This was his name before he became a player. When most players are spawned, he was made from the Rin of an NPC. Clarence the last blade was a child of a blacksmith named Norman the last blade. Norman was secretly the king of the world that they were on; not by Royal blood but by marriage he married the queen Anaya the last blade. She, being hungry for power and technically the only true ruler of their world, decided to kill the son and the father so she could hold on to her power and her magic, which would have made her immortal. The king, knowing this, left and ran away to a small village where he raised the child Clarence. Clarence, the last blade grew up as a normal child in a small village where he slowly learned swordplay until the day of his Father's death. When his father died, his father told him about his mother and how the throne was rightfully his. That he must train and take the throne. Which is what he would have done if it hadn't been that he woke up the next day on a completely different planet with no recollection of his past. He then discovered he had become what was called a player.

For the rest of his backstory, please consult his autobiography "The Way of the Player." To this day all he can remember is that he's a son, a blacksmith and almost nothing else. As well as no one knows why he became a player or what power from another dimension some and him to this world.
