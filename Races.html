Races

Overview

My game has several unique races, both magical and non-magical. Each of these races will be listed here, with an example image (if necesssary), and a detailed description.

Sand-Dwellers

Sand Dwellers, or Snow-Dwellers, or Garden-dwellers, or whatever variant they are, are a human-variant race, meaning that they appear human, and are almost entirely biologically human. However, sand dwellers are incredibly more temperature-resistant, meaning that they can walk through flames for short periods of times, as well as being capable of resisting freezing. Additionally, their feet are significanly wider, to compensate for living lives in loose sand. They generally live in deserts as they are one of the only races capable of living in those environments, so it essentially works as free land. They are capable of speaking to "Dirt", which includes dirt, sand, soil, gravel, non-processed salt (as it's found naturally), and even snow. The more powerful ones can use their powers to control these materials, depending on the environment they were raised and trained in.

Shelds

Shelds are a humanoid bird-like species. They are human shaped, and have mask-like structures that they wear, and cape-like wings. When flying, their arms appear to melt into their cloak, turning into wings. However, the cloak acts as a simple feather cloak when walking, which is how they spend the majority of their time. At birth, their cloak and masks are made of a wood-like material. However, the older they get, the harder and more reflective this material becomes. At adulthood, their bodies are made of a polished-stone looking material. The oldest of them have bodies made of the Mirror Metal. Their feathers are all made of the same materials, just as different densities and appearances. They are capable of removing these feathers and shaping them into new materials. Their feathers are also quite sharp, and can be thrown or used as a replacement for knives.

They live in their own nation in Errathis , and rarely have members of their culture outside. However, they are open and welcome to people living within their own cities, but they do not allow people to live in their own homes, due to [redacted].

Gemut

Gemut are a race of Gem-people. They are human-shaped, with their faces consisting of nothing other than their eyes, which are diamond-shaped. Their bodies are made of hard gem, but move as a human would. Gemut gain physical energy (essentiallly "calories") from magic. If they are without magical energy for extended periods of time, they will become weak, and could starve to death. They aren't able to eat food like humans. Their bodies are protected from most physical attacks that don't have sufficient force, as their bodies are roughly as hard as Hasper, or for easier terms, Granite, meaning that they would easily break bone or stone, but could be chipped down with metal. Their bodies use magic to naturally regenerate wounds, such as when pieces of them break off. This means that repeatedly breaking them would cause them to become hungry, and could therefore starve them to death. However, their entire body could be broken, which would kill them. The pieces that break off are extremely sharp, and could be used as a knife for lack of better resources. Their hair grows in the shapes that it is broken, forming into potentially complex shapes. It can be broken down into shorter shapes if wanted.

Gemut are each made of a different gem, the power-level and rarity of the gem determining the likelyhood of them being born. They are made from Ancient Gems. More powerful types are less common, because they need more energy to remain alive, and therefore die faster. Additionally, the types are recessive, meaning they are less common to come out genetically.

They lve primarily in normal society, being native to Lotusium. They are generally in groups, rather than a solo Gemut living in a society.

There is a variety of Gemut, called a half-gemut. They have the powers (and some durability) of a Gemut, but the appearance and dietary needs of a human. They are human, with slightly tinted skin to match their Gemut heritage, with semi-transparent, pointed ears, and shards of a glass-like material in their hair, which appear similar to horns. These can be broken and shaved down similar to Gemut hair.

Grout

Grout are a race of shapeshifters. Originally, there were only 196 (14^2) Grout, each with varying intelligence and shapeshifting capability. Each one was able to duplicate themselves into more Grout, making additional versions of themselves with the same DNA. Each one can recreate the material of any non-magical material, otherwise being any material known to Earth. However, the more powerful ones can recreate magical metals, and the more intelligent ones can become complicated mechanisms. Each clone has the same intelligence and power as the previous, but they do not have the same memories, essentially reproducing. Grout are native to Lotusium , but they have traveled all over the universe.

Grout cannot get nutrition from normal food, but they can (and do) enjoy eating it. However, they require a specific type of magical energy for sustenance, which can be made with Brein or Essence. Without this, they can starve to death.
