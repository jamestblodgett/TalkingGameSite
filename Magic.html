Magic

Overview

In my game, there are plenty of unique types of magic, each with the same essential rules:

  • Magic requires "Energy", which comes in various forms depending on the type of magic. Running out of energy or gaining more than your capacity both have extreme consequences.
  • Magic is directly connected into your soul, through something known as a Spark. Each spark can only hold one type of magic, unless that type is an accessory, such as using magical items or bonds to access magic.
