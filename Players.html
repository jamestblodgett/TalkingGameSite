Players

This is the main page for the Players of my game - the players being all the people who play the game. This will include character sheets, power sets, and additional information. Each one of these is a character who is made an used by a real person, and has both hard work on my end and on their end. Some of these have years of experience playing the game, while others are just getting started.

If you do not see your name listed here, it is because I do not want to list your name on this site without consent. If you want to be listed here, contact me and tell me to add you.

These names are listed because this is their character-name, NOT their real name.
