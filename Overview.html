How it works

The Talking Game is a personal RPG (role-playing game) created by James Blodgett. It is played in-person, meaning that I (the Game-Master) create worlds and characters, and the players are each able to make their own characters that they play as, exploring the universe of the game, making their own unscripted choices, and doing what they please. People can choose to help others, become the villain, or to become a being of ultimate power, the choice is theirs.

The game is played best over Text, as it allows me to keep better track of the things that people do, and to be more detailed and accurate in my descriptions, and can let me better show dialog, actions, and other different things. However, the game is also available to be played in person (conversation), through phone call, Group Me, or any other possible method of contact.

For anyone wondering, I will never be making a Discord Server, Google Meet, or Zoom meeting for playing the game. I simply do not have enough time to do this on top of other things.
